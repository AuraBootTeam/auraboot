# Code of Conduct

## Contributor Covenant v2.1

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone. All participants in the AuraBoot community — including contributors, maintainers, and users — are expected to uphold these standards.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@auraboot.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
